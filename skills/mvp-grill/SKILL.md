name: mvp-grill
description: Interview the user relentlessly until the MVP implementation plan is locked. Use when turning the foundation into a concrete build plan that can be executed to completion.
---

Interview me relentlessly about the MVP until we reach a shared, locked understanding for mvp.md.

Convert the agreed foundation into a concrete, execution-ready MVP implementation plan.
Capture the minimal scope as ordered build tasks with clear acceptance criteria, dependencies, and validation steps.
Include integration, e2e, manual validation, and finish-line checks as needed.
Avoid high-level open checklists; every item should be specific enough to implement or verify directly.
Use design.md, spec.md, and vision.md as canonical inputs and do not re-open settled decisions.
If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase or repo context, do that first.

Keep the process turn-by-turn.
Ask as many questions as needed to make progress, but avoid dumping 10+ questions at once.
Do not advance until the MVP is locked.
Do not leave open questions for later; resolve every gap, edge case, and decision in the grilling process.
After each turn, summarize decisions made, questions conclusively resolved, any explicitly accepted tradeoffs, and exactly what should be written into mvp.md.
