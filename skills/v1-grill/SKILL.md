name: v1-grill
description: Interview the user relentlessly until the v1 implementation plan is locked. Use when turning the MVP foundation into a concrete next-build plan that can be executed to completion.
---

Interview me relentlessly about v1 until we reach a shared, locked understanding for v1.md.

Expand the MVP into a concrete, execution-ready v1 implementation plan.
Capture the next meaningful increment as ordered build tasks with clear acceptance criteria, dependencies, and validation steps.
Avoid high-level open checklists; every item should be specific enough to implement or verify directly.
Keep the plan grounded in the already-locked vision, spec, design, and MVP docs.
Use earlier docs as canonical inputs and do not re-open settled decisions.
If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase or repo context, do that first.

Keep the process turn-by-turn.
Ask as many questions as needed to make progress, but avoid dumping 10+ questions at once.
Do not advance until v1 is locked.
Do not leave open questions for later; resolve every gap, edge case, and decision in the grilling process.
After each turn, summarize decisions made, questions conclusively resolved, any explicitly accepted tradeoffs, and exactly what should be written into v1.md.
